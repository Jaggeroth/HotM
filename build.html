Build Instructions

The conceit is that each player comes from their own reality. So when it comes to creating your own character you also get to describe your own world. However there are somethings all must share or questions your origin must answer:

  • Most supers are villians so you are outnumbered.
  • You have been fighting the good fight for a while, so how have you survived? Are you a good all-rounder? Lucky? Or something else? Maybe you are not surviving? Perhaps you know your time is running out?
  • Supers are unpopular with the population at large. You are no exception.
  • How have you stayed hidden? Assuming you are?
  • You are based out of either London or New York (but feel free to rename them).
  • Your chosen city is the world's hotspot of super activity.
  • Supers (at least publicly) have only started appearing in the 21st Century.

Optional things you may wish to consider:

  • Any useful contacts you may have?
  • Any colourful villians you have fought, maybe defeated. Maybe even killed.
  • What do you do outside of heroing? I am highly likely to give a free bonus to be spent on skills.
